Famous World

A responsive map of famous people in the history of world based on data from PANTHEON and Wikipedia.

This project was created for COM-480: Data Visualization class on EPFL by the proud team:

  • Ravinithesh Annapureddy
  • Michal Bien

You can select the prefered timespan and profession to narrow down the list of famous people to your search needs. Then, explore the historical information by clicking on the photo marker. It's as simple as that!
